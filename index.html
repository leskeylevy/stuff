Brush Tu is an artist Collective based in BuruBuru comprosed of 12 visual Artists. Established in 2013, the main objective of coming together was to harness the artistic potential of the member artists and utilize their prowess to deliver collaborative works of art and be a major conributor in the elevation of Kenyan Art.
